I live in

In this blog, I will talk about my hobby: reading. I started reading for pleasure during the quarantine in 2019 out of boredom. I had nothing to do, so I picked up a random book I had bought years ago, and ended up really enjoying it.

As time went on, I was reading everyday, and began to be interested in trying other genres. So far, I've only been into fiction, but my goal is to expand my reading taste and find a non-fiction book that resonates with me.

Now, I try to read 30 books a year, which may not sound like much, but as someone who couldn't get through 15 pages without getting bored, I'm proud of my progress. Reading as a hobby has helped me improve my comprehension skills, which I believe is essential and will be very useful in the future.
